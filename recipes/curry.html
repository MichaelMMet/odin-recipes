Curry

What will be made

We will be making a classic and simply divine Japaense Curry that will be sure to delite the tastebuds of all. Japanese curry usually is less spicy than traditional Indian curry allowing many more to enjoy it.

Ingedients needed

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 3-4 Cubed Potatoes
  • 3 Chopped Carrots
  • 1 sliced Onion
  • Rice
  • Curry Roux

Steps

  1. Chop up the Potatoes, Carrots, and Onions (set aside)
  2. Add the Onions in with some oil to caramelize
  3. Cut the chicken into sizes to your liking
  4. Wash the rice and get the rice going in your rice cooker
  5. Add the other ingredients to the pot and cover with water
  6. Add 2 cubes of Curry Roux of your flavor of choice and mix it in
  7. Simmer for 10 minutes while stirring
  8. Your curry is ready!
