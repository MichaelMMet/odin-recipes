KBBQ

Picture of a wonderful KBBQ meal!

There is not one specific recipe for KBBQ but that does not mean there are not certain aspects that a KBBQ must have!

The few things you must have include:

  1. A Grill
  2. A good helping of rice
  3. Some slices of your favorite (prefeably marinated) meat such as Brisket
  4. Some sides such as Kimchi or Kongnamul Muchim

This was just a guideline to get you started. Now go off, invite some friends, add your flair, and have some amazing KBBQ!
